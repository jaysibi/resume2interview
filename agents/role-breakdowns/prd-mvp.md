# Product Requirements Document (PRD) — MVP

## Overview
The MVP will deliver an AI-powered resume optimization engine that helps job seekers tailor their resumes to specific job descriptions and maximize their chances of passing ATS filters and recruiter screening.

## User Stories

### 1. Resume Upload and Parsing
- **As a job seeker, I want to upload my resume (PDF/DOCX) so that the system can extract my skills, experience, and achievements into structured data.**
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Resume is parsed with high accuracy; user sees extracted data for review.

### 2. Job Description (JD) Upload and Parsing
- **As a job seeker, I want to upload or paste a job description so that the system can extract required skills and keywords.**
- **Acceptance Criteria:** JD is parsed and key requirements are displayed.

### 3. Skill and Keyword Extraction
- **As a job seeker, I want the system to extract and display skills and keywords from both my resume and the JD so I can see how well they match.**
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Skills/keywords are clearly listed and categorized.

### 4. Gap Analysis and ATS Match Scoring
- **As a job seeker, I want to see a score and summary of how well my resume matches the JD, including missing keywords and suggestions.**
- **Acceptance Criteria:** ATS score and gap report are generated and explained.

### 5. Achievement/Bullet Rewriting and Enhancement
- **As a job seeker, I want weak resume bullets to be rewritten with strong action verbs and quantifiable outcomes, optimized for ATS and recruiters.**
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Rewritten bullets are clear, concise, and factually accurate.

### 6. ATS-safe Resume Formatting and Export
- **As a job seeker, I want my optimized resume exported in ATS-safe DOCX/PDF format.**
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Exported resume passes ATS checks and is visually clean.

### 7. ATS Score and Keyword Report Generation
- **As a job seeker, I want a report showing my ATS score, missing keywords, and recruiter-style feedback.**
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Report is clear, actionable, and downloadable.

## Out of Scope
- Multi-language support
- Integration with external job boards
- Advanced analytics/dashboard

## Success Metrics
- Resume parsing accuracy ≥ 90%
- User satisfaction ≥ 80% (survey)
- ATS score improvement for ≥ 80% of users
