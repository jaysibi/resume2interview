# Technical Team Validation Checklist — MVP

## 1. Requirements & User Stories
- Are all user stories clear, actionable, and testable?
- Do the requirements cover all core MVP features?
- Are there any missing edge cases or scenarios?

## 2. Acceptance Criteria
- Is each acceptance criterion specific and measurable?
- Can all criteria be validated through testing or demo?
- Are there any ambiguous or conflicting criteria?

## 3. Architecture & Feasibility
- Is the proposed architecture feasible within the 2-day sprint?
- Are the technology stack and tools agreed upon?
- Are there any technical risks or blockers?

## 4. Task Breakdown & Ownership
- Are all tasks assigned to the appropriate roles?
- Is the workload balanced and achievable in the timeline?
- Are dependencies and handoffs clearly defined?

## 5. Communication & Escalation
- Are daily standups and checkpoints scheduled?
- Is there a clear process for escalating blockers?

## 6. Go/No-Go Decision
- Does the technical team agree the MVP can be delivered in 2 days?
- Are all stakeholders aligned on scope and priorities?

---

**Action:** Review this checklist with the Technical Architect, AI Engineer, and Software Developer before starting the sprint. Document any concerns or required adjustments.
